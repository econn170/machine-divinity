Ethics & Content Warning

Please note that this project does not aim to amplify harmful ideology. Posts were filtered for mystical, spiritual, and psychosis-coded language—not slurs or targeted content. The resulting models are intentionally small and unstable, serving as artistic and analytic objects rather than polished chatbots. The goal is to interrogate how online communities collectively construct symbolic worlds, not to reproduce or legitimize harmful viewpoints.

However, the models do not censor profanity or "harmful" keywords, as this project is not interested in defining such parameters and seeks only to create a truly representative model of the data. Therefore, the oracle’s responses may be offensive or unsettling to some — please keep this in mind while prompting.
