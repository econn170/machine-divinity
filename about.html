About the Project

Machine Divinity is a computational-artistic exploration of spiritual and apocolyptic language across anonymous online spaces.

The project analyzes 4chan discourse through an eschatological framework and trains three generative “oracles” based on distinct linguistic worldviews.

The website interface allows visitors to interact with these oracles, prompting them to speak in the distinctive style of their eschatological category.
