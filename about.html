About the Project

Machine Divinity is a computational-artistic exploration of spiritual and apocolyptic language across anonymous online spaces.

The website interface allows visitors to interact with three oracles, prompting them to speak in the style of their eschatological category. These "oracles" generate their content from three language models, trained on 9,383 4chan posts scraped for spiritual and psychosis-coded language.
